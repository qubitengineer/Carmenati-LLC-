About Carmenati LLC

Founders

Carmenati LLC was founded by David Carmenate and Magdel Carmenate, two Cuban-born professionals with diverse and complementary backgrounds.

David Carmenate

David holds a dual Bachelor’s degree in Philosophy and Economics. With extensive research experience in finance and legal topics, he brings analytical rigor and strategic insight to the firm's operations.

Magdel Carmenate

Magdel is a Political Scientist with a strong background in customer service. Her interpersonal skills and client-first mindset ensure that Carmenati LLC delivers human-centered consulting solutions.

Both founders previously worked at a law firm for over a year, having handled hundreds of cases together. They are both bilingual and share a commitment to serving diverse communities.
