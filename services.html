Our Services

At Carmenati LLC, we offer a full suite of consulting solutions tailored to guide individuals, entrepreneurs, and enterprises through every stage of their journey—from inception to expansion. Our expertise spans business, finance, law, and strategy, ensuring that your vision is backed by insight, structure, and execution.

Company Formation

Starting a business is more than paperwork—it’s laying the foundation for your legacy. We help you choose the right structure (LLC, Corporation, Partnership, etc.), file the proper documentation, navigate local, state, and federal regulations, and secure all required licenses and permits so you can launch with confidence.

Financial Services

From startups to scaling firms, financial clarity is everything. We offer tailored budgeting, financial forecasting, cash flow optimization, and debt restructuring to keep your business solvent, strategic, and prepared for growth.

Credit Management

Whether you’re rebuilding credit or negotiating debt, we deliver customized solutions to assess, repair, and monitor your credit profile—empowering both personal and business financial strength.

Legal Advisory

Our legal consulting ensures your business operates on solid ground. We assist with contract drafting and review, regulatory compliance, dispute resolution, corporate governance, and intellectual property protection—so you’re protected at every turn.

Tax Compliance

Tax season doesn’t have to be stressful. We handle tax planning, preparation, and filing at both the state and federal level, offer audit representation, and help implement strategies to legally minimize your tax burden.

Real Estate Advisory

Whether acquiring, selling, or restructuring property, we guide you through every phase. From title adjustments and deed changes to investment analysis and lease negotiations, our advisors help you make smart, strategic real estate moves.

Accounting Services

Accurate records are the heartbeat of a healthy business. Our accounting services include meticulous bookkeeping, payroll processing, AR/AP management, and audit preparation—ensuring compliance and financial clarity year-round.

Investment Planning

We provide comprehensive wealth and portfolio guidance—from retirement planning and asset diversification to risk mitigation strategies—designed to grow and protect your financial future.

Project Planning

Every successful project starts with a plan. We conduct feasibility studies, manage timelines and resources, identify risks, and track performance, so your initiative stays on schedule and delivers measurable results.

Additional Services

We go beyond the expected. Our team offers strategic market analysis, process optimization, succession planning, and M&A support—driving long-term value and transformative growth across your enterprise.

Immigration Services

We provide comprehensive immigration services, including assistance with visa applications, green card filings, citizenship processes, and other legal documentation. Our expert team guides you through paperwork, ensures accurate filings, and offers personalized support to navigate complex immigration procedures efficiently.

Notary Public Services

Reliable notarization for legal documents, contracts, affidavits, and more. Ensuring authenticity and compliance for your important paperwork.
